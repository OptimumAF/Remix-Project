The Impact of Unity's Per-User Install Charging Plan on Market Revenue

Unity Technologies has long been a mainstay in the game creation business, providing developers with an easily accessible and flexible game engine that allows them to make a wide range of creative and inventive games. In the gaming sector, Unity's recent plan to pay game developers for each install made by a user has caused debate and alarm.

Unity's new pricing strategy would drastically reduce its market revenue by driving away developers, upsetting the industry, and alienating its core clientele, all of which will undermine the company's lucrative revenue sources.

Through its creative revenue methods in addition to its technology offerings, Unity Technologies has become a mainstay in the game business. Cuofano claims that Unity first had a dual business model that was based on asset store earnings and subscription payments (Cuofano). This method carefully considered and balanced the interests of all parties involved in the game creation process, from independent developers to major studios.
